OUR FOOD

At Three Guys And A Grill, the food is the heart and soul of the dining experience. With a focus on hardwood charcoal grilling, every dish infuses rich, smoky flavors that tantalize the taste buds. From juicy sausages to succulent grilled meats, each item on the menu is carefully crafted to perfection.

Burgers

Our burgers are a testament to our dedication to quality and flavor. Each burger is made with premium beef, seasoned to perfection, and grilled over an open flame to achieve that perfect char and smoky taste. Whether you prefer the classic Anghang Burger, the savory Texas Burger, or the unique flavors of our Tommy Burger, every bite promises a delightful experience.

Drinks

To complement our hearty meals, we offer a selection of refreshing drinks. Our homemade sodas, infused with natural fruit flavors, provide a delightful alternative to traditional soft drinks. Favorites include our classic Coke, the sugar-free Coke Zero, and the ever-popular Sprite. For those who prefer something lighter, we also offer refreshing bottled water.

Hotdogs

Our hotdogs are not to be missed. Made with a blend of premium meats and unique seasoning blends, they are grilled to perfection over an open flame. Try our Bird Hotdog for a taste of our smoky, juicy perfection, or indulge in the rich flavors of our Posh Hotdog. The Swiss Hotdog and Thunder Hotdog are also favorites among our guests.

Sides

No meal is complete without our delicious sides. From crispy fries to fresh salads, our sides are the perfect complement to our grilled specialties. Each side is prepared with the same attention to quality and flavor that defines our main dishes.

Whether you're dining in our cozy restaurant or enjoying our catering services at an event, Three Guys And A Grill delivers a memorable culinary experience that keeps guests coming back for more. Our commitment to quality ingredients and expert grilling techniques has earned us a reputation as one of the best grill houses in town.
